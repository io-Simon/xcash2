Empowering You: Take Control of Your Funds with XCASH Tools

Flex-Private Send

With XCASH, a single wallet can handle both public and private transactions, providing anonymity when privacy is paramount, and transparency when necessary.

Flex-Private Receive

XCASH offers the flexibility to receive exclusively private or public transactions to an address. In addition, unconditional transactions are set as the default, much like private send.

Private Stake

For an enhanced user experience, XCASH offers private staking, a feature that is exclusive to our blockchain.

Secured & Unfalsifiable

XCASH secures private and public transactions through cryptographically-signed, fixed-size ring transactions, creating an unfalsifiable record.
