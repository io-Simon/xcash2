Foundation

A non-profit org that coordinates the development of XCASH.

Open-Source

XCASH is an open-source project, built upon the work of skilled and engaged contributors. Every project around the XCASH Ecosystem is open for contributions by the public GitHub.

Community Driven

Following a clear line of technical development, the vision is built with the community, by the community. Every project proposed by the community is assessed and evaluated by the foundation to provide funding.

Community Funded

The project startup costs were provided by the founding team, without an ICO. The current development of the cryptocurrency is now paid with the community fund, established to reward projects built for the XCASH ecosystem.
