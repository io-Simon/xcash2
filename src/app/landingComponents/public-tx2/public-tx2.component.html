Pub X

It's simple and easy to use, even for beginners.

Public Transactions Only

Choose to receive only Public transactions to your wallet address. This means your transactions are visible to everyone on the network and can be tracked by anyone.

Unique Transaction Names

With our system, you can give your transactions a unique name using a Public (pxcash) address. This makes it easy to track and manage your transactions without any complexity.

Multiple Address Options

When you register a Public name, you get access to three different addresses: Public (pxcash), Private (sxcash), and Regular (xcash). This gives you the flexibility to choose the right address for your needs.

Simplify Your Transactions

Our Public TX 2.0 system is designed to simplify complex transaction challenges. Join our community today and start enjoying easy, secure transactions!
