X Pay

Bringing Crypto Wallets to Social Media

Who

Our instant and private peer-to-peer exchange of value features are beneficial for gaming communities, sports betting, collaborative chat, online dating, MOOCs, and even crypto forecasters.

Merchant Service

Our X-Payment wallet offers advanced tools for social media monetization. With our merchant system, users can monetize their status in communities and create unique microeconomies.

Instant Secure Payments

X-Payment's infrastructure is built on proven security protocols, with funds primarily stored in cold wallets. Our off-chain payments are instant and come with hybrid privacy features.

Current Platforms

Our platform is currently available on Discord, Twitter, and other platforms to be announced.
