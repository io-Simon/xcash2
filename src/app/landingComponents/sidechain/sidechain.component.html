Sidechains

Personal Blockchains on Demand.

No hardware

A sidechain is a full blockchain that is hosted by our delegates. This means that contrary to traditional blockchains, you do not need to rent your own infrastructure.

No code

Setting up a sidechain is easy. Our X-Blockchain modules offer pre-customized options to fit your requirements.

Secure

All nodes operate side chains with implicit consent from all other nodes as part of XCASH's inherited security model.

Improvement

Delegate nodes compete to secure the best resources to process and store transactions quickly. This helps ensure fast and secure decentralized transactions.

Custom

Our sidechains are flexible and cost-effective. You can choose how many delegates and sidechains you need.
