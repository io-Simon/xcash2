XCASH Cold Storage & Cross-Chain Swaps.

Bridging The Gap

The bridge from the XCASH Mainnet to the Polygon Network is Swap Shop: the free and secure XCASH storage solution. Swap your Mainnet XCASH to Polygon XCASH, instantly.

Secure

Store & Use
