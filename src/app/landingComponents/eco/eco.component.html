ECO X

Revolutionizing Energy Consumption in Blockchain

The Revolution

XCASH stands out from Proof-of-Work and POS networks with its minimal energy consumption. Unlike energy-intensive mining or wallet usage, XCASH eliminates these needs and provides a sustainable platform for developing blockchain applications.

Eco-Proof

Eco-Proof technology eliminates the need for devices to remain open to stake, instead allowing the voted-for delegate to distribute rewards. The reward status can be easily verified through our delegate explorer API.

The Truth

Our commitment to sustainability is evident in our energy-efficient blockchain, which is estimated to consume less energy than four toasters.
