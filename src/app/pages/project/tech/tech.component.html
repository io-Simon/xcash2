Tech

Our Mission Is To Make Web3 Private

Hybrid Transactions

We are offering the transparency of bitcoin transactions with Monero's obfuscation to allow most privacy needs to be addressed.

DPOPS

We have built a variant of the dPoS consensus protocol:
- Privacy-switch for stake used in the voting process
- Byzantine Fault Tolerant algorithm
- Verifiable Random Functions for the block producer selection process

Sidechains & Smart Contracts

We're developing our layer-2 solution to enhance our layer-1 technology with more scalability and use cases. Our delegates can host sidechains and execute smart contracts for additional yields, increasing the value of our ecosystem.
