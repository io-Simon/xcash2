Smart Contracts

Our Smart Contracts approach combines Monero's obfuscation and Bitcoin's transparency for unrivaled privacy. This sets a new standard for blockchain security and confidentiality.

Sidechains

Our cutting-edge approach enhances scalability, flexibility, and privacy by enabling off-chain transactions while maintaining the security of the main blockchain.

NFTs

NFT integration in our layer-2 offers new monetization and interaction opportunities with digital assets. It provides unique asset representation, exciting possibilities for creators, collectors, and investors.

Current

Sidechain Payments

On the same basis of the Lightning Network on Bitcoin, sidechain payments enable quicker payment processing on the X-Cash blockchain.

Done

WXCASH

Wrapped XCASH is an ERC20 bridge for X-Cash, integrating X-Cash with Ethereum and Polygon. X Swap's secure platform is developing a solution to swap XCASH for WXCASH on Polygon.

Flexprivacy

Flexprivacy is a unique feature enabling both public and private transactions on the same network, on a per-transaction basis. Every transaction made public is viewable on the X-Cash explorer in a similar fashion as on the Bitcoin chain.

DPOPS

Delegated-Proof-Of-Private-Stake (DPOPS) is a new blockchain consensus developed on top of the cryptonote protocol to tackle the caveats of POW consensus.
