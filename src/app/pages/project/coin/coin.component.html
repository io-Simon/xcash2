Understanding Coin Distribution & Reserve-Proof Verification

60%

XCASH are minted at every block creation by elected delegates. The reward from block creation is redistributed to voters as a ratio of their vote.

14.2%

Has been distributed through a two-year long airdrop program, starting in 2018.
Now completed, these coins are part of the circulating supply.

10%

These funds have been used by the company to further the development of XCASH. All decisions about the unlocking of these funds will be made public.

5%

The private investors fund was designed to enable large quantity investments of XCASH in the initial steps of the project. It has now been mostly depleted.

5%

These rewards are locked until several market capitalization milestones are reached. Every time the market capitalization of XCASH is multiply by 10, starting at 10M USD market cap, 20% of the reward is unlocked.

5.8%

The contributor fund is used for funding projects, bounties, and specific development tasks of the XCASH project.
This fund is managed by the XCASH Foundation
